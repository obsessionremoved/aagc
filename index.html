Group Conscience Meeting - Cornerstone Fellowship 6am

I will only be acting as an impartial facilitator, I will not voice my own opinions on the topics.

I will be keeping track of time. I have invited Simon H. to act as our recorder.

  • We will read over the topic summary.
  • Discussion: I will call on one member at a time to share their thoughts without crosstalk for up to 1 minute. Anyone can pass.
  • We will have a second round of sharing if anyone has anything NEW to add. (up to 30 seconds)
  • We will take a vote. (if necessary)
  • We will hear from any members who had a minority opinion. (up to 1 min)
  • If anyone has changed their mind we will take a final vote which will stand.
  • Substantial unanimity (2/3 majority) is needed for the proposal to pass.
  • The recorder may relay their notes, and make changes if necessary.
